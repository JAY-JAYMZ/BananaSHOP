Breaking News

Apple Increases iPhone Production in India

Apple has significantly ramped up its iPhone production in India, accounting for approximately 14% of its global output, according to recent reports. The tech giant has assembled iPhones worth $14 billion in the country during the 2024 fiscal year, marking a substantial increase from the previous year. This expansion is seen as a strategic move by Apple to diversify its production base and tap into the growing Indian market.

Apple Faces Legal Action Over Alleged Leaks by Former Employee

Apple has filed a lawsuit against a former employee, accusing him of leaking sensitive information to the media and other tech companies. The lawsuit, filed in the Superior Court of California, alleges that the former employee, Andrew Aude, disclosed confidential details about several unreleased Apple products, including the Vision Pro mixed reality headset. Apple is seeking damages exceeding $25,000, claiming that Aude's actions have harmed the company's ability to surprise and delight customers with new product releases.

Apple Lays Off Over 600 Employees Amid Project Cancellations

Apple has confirmed the layoff of more than 600 employees following the cancellation of two major projects: a self-driving car and a smartwatch display. The layoffs, which occurred at multiple locations in California, are a direct result of the company's decision to terminate these projects due to various challenges and uncertainties. Affected employees were reportedly working on these initiatives, and some of them will be offered opportunities to transfer to other teams within the company.
